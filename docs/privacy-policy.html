隱私權政策

最後更新日期：2026 年 3 月 1 日

資料收集與儲存

Duckout 是一款娛樂用途的 App，不具備真實撥打、接聽或簡訊通訊功能。本 App 重視您的隱私，所有遊戲資料（進度、設定等）皆儲存於您的裝置本機，不會上傳至任何伺服器。本 App 不需要建立帳號，也不會收集您的姓名、電子郵件或其他個人身分資訊。

分析資料

本 App 不會收集任何使用分析資料。我們不使用任何第三方分析工具，您的使用行為完全屬於您自己。

購買紀錄

App 內購買透過 Apple 的 StoreKit 框架處理。所有交易由 Apple 管理，我們不會存取您的付款資訊。購買狀態僅儲存於您的裝置上。

第三方服務

本 App 使用以下第三方服務：

  • Apple StoreKit — App 內購買處理

該服務受 Apple 的隱私權政策約束，建議您參閱相關文件。

兒童隱私

本 App 不會故意收集 13 歲以下兒童的個人資訊。如果您發現有兒童在未經家長同意的情況下提供了個人資訊，請聯繫我們，我們將立即刪除相關資料。

隱私權政策變更

我們可能會不定期更新本隱私權政策。任何變更將在本頁面公佈，建議您定期查看。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Data Collection & Storage

Duckout is an entertainment app and does not provide real calling, answering, or SMS communication functionality. We value your privacy. All game data (progress, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server. This app does not require account creation and does not collect your name, email, or any other personally identifiable information.

Analytics

This app does not collect any usage analytics data. We do not use any third-party analytics tools, and your usage behavior remains entirely your own.

Purchase Records

In-app purchases are handled through Apple's StoreKit framework. All transactions are managed by Apple, and we do not access your payment information. Purchase status is stored only on your device.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — In-app purchase processing

This service is subject to Apple's privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that a child has provided personal information without parental consent, please contact us and we will promptly delete the data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
