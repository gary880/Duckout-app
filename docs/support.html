常見問題

Q：Duckout 會收集我的個人資料嗎？

不會。Duckout 所有資料都儲存在您的裝置本機，不會上傳至任何伺服器，也不需要建立帳號。

Q：需要網路才能使用嗎？

不需要。所有藉口內容都內建在 App 中，完全離線即可使用。

Q：如何恢復已購買的內容？

前往 App 內的設定頁面，點擊「恢復購買」即可透過您的 Apple ID 恢復先前的購買紀錄。

Q：我的收藏和日曆紀錄會同步到其他裝置嗎？

目前資料僅儲存在本機裝置，暫不支援跨裝置同步。刪除 App 將會清除所有本機資料。

Q：模擬來電功能會真的撥打電話嗎？

不會。模擬來電僅為畫面與聲音模擬，不會產生任何真實通話或費用。

Q：App 適合什麼年齡使用？

Duckout 的內容分級為 13+，建議 13 歲以上使用。所有藉口僅供娛樂用途。

FAQ

Q: Does Duckout collect my personal data?

No. All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and no account creation is required.

Q: Do I need internet to use the app?

No. All excuse content is built into the app and works completely offline.

Q: How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the Settings page in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" to recover previous purchases via your Apple ID.

Q: Will my favorites and calendar sync across devices?

Currently, data is stored locally only and does not sync across devices. Deleting the app will erase all local data.

Q: Does the fake call feature make real phone calls?

No. The fake call feature only simulates a call screen and sound. It does not initiate any real calls or incur any charges.

Q: What age group is this app for?

Duckout is rated 13+ and recommended for ages 13 and above. All excuses are intended for entertainment purposes only.
